Graph 2: Spending Efficiency

This graph visualizes data from the U.S. Education Department. Every year, almost all the states increase budget for public schools. This graph compares all the states of America and shows how effective they were in increasing the budget and seeing improved math scores by 8th graders from 1992-2016.

For each state, the efficiency numbers were generated with this formula:
(Avg math score in 2016 - Avg math score in 1992) / (expenditure in 2016 - expenditure in 1992)
Note that the dollar amount of 1992 was adjusted for inflation to year 2016.

This gives us math scores increase per dollar. Finally, before graphing the efficiency numbers, they were scaled between 0 and 1 on the color gradient.
